About Idaho

Idaho is in the Pacific Northwest of the United States of America. It has a rich history with “Manifest Destiny” and the United States Expansion into the west. It is an extremely scenic state with multiple mountains, river, lakes, and fields. This along with its abundance of natural resources is why Idaho is often referred to as the “gem state”. This also is why Idaho is home to numerous national parks including Yellowstone National Park which it shares with Wyoming and Montana, Craters of the Moon National Monument, and the Hagerman Fossil Beds National Monument. All these natural landmarks and features make Idaho outdoor recreational community on of the best in the nation with activities such as whitewater rafting, fishing, skiing, and snowboarding. In fact in the state is such a good location for outdoor recreation that in 2009 it hosted the Winter Special Olympics.
